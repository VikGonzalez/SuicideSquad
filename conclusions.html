Conclusions

This topic has a lot of ground to cover and its sensitivity can be a limitation factor to dive deep in it, or to completely understand it. The data on suicides is deficient because of its reliability of registration in some countries. There are problems with the accuracy of the official figures made available to WHO. This cause of death is sometimes hidden and underreported for different reasons.

For starters it is not as simple as if someone took their own life or not. It separates in suicide ideation, have a suicide plan and attempting actual suicide.

This individual fields depend on the methods, reasons, intention, economic issues, mental disorders, substance abuse, education, country, among a lot of factors. Specifically, in Mexico it is shown that the rates have grown the last 8 years. What has also increased is the unemployment and obesity in adults and children. This sectors along with the importance of social media, beauty and work stereotypes, etc.
