Nordic countries have repeatedly come up on the top 3 happiest countries on Earth. One would expect that with colder temperatures and higher rates of suicide, their happiness index would take a hit but that is not the case! Turns out there are more underlying indicators that generate higher happiness indices: a well-functioning democracy, generous and effective social welfare benefits, low levels of crime and corruption, and satisfied citizens who feel free and trust each other and governmental institutions. A strong sense of community and unity within the population is a crucial component of happy countries. How does your country compare to the top scorers?
